Introducing the HBUK + Saifcam ecosystem

SafeSound is about turning real life into structured, verifiable records you control. Saifcam captures what happens around you. HBUK anchors those events in an immutable truth book, so nothing can be quietly changed later.

What is HBUK?

HBUK is a private, append-only journal. Each entry is hashed and signed, and every day we publish a single daily seal so anyone can verify which entries existed that day. This uses straightforward cryptography, not a blockchain.

Where Saifcam fits

Saifcam handles the vision input. V0 uses hosted Gemini Flash for short clips and structured summaries, with a path toward compact on-device models so processing can stay private on your own hardware.

Privacy modes you can choose

Me Only

Your private journal. No training and no raw media leaves your devices; only short sealed text is stored.

Private on the Edge

Small models run on your camera, hub, or phone so intelligence stays with you.

License to market

Opt-in commercial licensing for selected events on your terms (see the Privacy page for details).

Why this matters

Most systems hoard raw media. We keep what is useful and verifiable instead—short, high-fidelity entries with cryptographic receipts—so you can build personal intelligence, prove compliance, or license value without giving up control.
