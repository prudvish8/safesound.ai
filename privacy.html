Privacy & Data-Rights

You own your data. Choose how it's used.

A. License-to-market

  • Export allowed under a commercial license
  • Payouts per accepted event
  • Revocable for future exports

B. Private-model-only

  • No external export
  • Tenant-local model training only
  • Better private models over time

C. Utility-only

  • No training usage
  • Event-centric storage
  • Strict retention windows

Retention

Default retention is event-centric. For Utility-only, data is retained minimally to provide the service. For Private-model-only, training artifacts remain within your tenant. For License-to-market, export copies are governed by the license and your revocation for future exports.
